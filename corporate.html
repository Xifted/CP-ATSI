ABOUT US

PT. ATLANTIS SERVICES INDONESIA (ASI) is a national private company which established in 2007. Well-engaged in inspection or verification services for various types of products/goods resulting from industry and also trade. ASI have experiences in inspection activities of manufactured industrial products in the form of raw materials and/or auxiliary materials, intermediate products (Intermediate Goods) and final products/finished goods/consumer goods (Consumers Goods). To meet the customer needs, such as inspection activities of manufactured products can be carried out in a series of production processes starting from the initial inspection before production (Initial Checks), during the production process (during production), and inspection of the final product (Final Random Inspection). ASI has succeeded in building a global service networks through cooperations and collaborations with various inspection company partners in various countries in the world to carry out activities in order to meet the needs and interests of the customers
